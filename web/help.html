How to Use the Hotel Management System

Step 1: Open Login Page
Navigate to the login page of the system using your browser.
Step 2: Enter Credentials
Enter your Username and Password in the respective fields.
Step 3: Validate Inputs
Click the Login button. The system will validate your input. - Empty fields or short passwords will trigger a popup alert.
Step 4: Login
If credentials are correct, you will be redirected to the Dashboard. If incorrect, a popup will appear saying "Incorrect Username or Password".
Step 5: Dashboard Navigation
From the dashboard, you can access various modules such as: - View Reservations - Add New Reservation - View Bills - Manage Rooms
Step 6: Manage Reservations
- To view all reservations, go to the Reservations section. - To update a reservation, click Edit next to the reservation. - To delete a reservation, click Delete.
Step 7: Billing
- Select a reservation and click View Bill to see the charges. - You can print or save the bill as needed.
